How to use Fast Facts Simulator

Updated: documents Settings (gear), Mode cycling, Timer Off, Auto Move, Scoreboard visibility, and Tournament Readiness.

  1. Choose filters: open Category to pick topics. Filters are in the header; advanced controls live under the Settings ▾ (gear) at the far right.
  2. Mode (Settings): inside Settings the Mode button cycles Easy → Medium → Hard → Tournament. Switching modes resets saved scores. Tournament locks filters and enforces competitive defaults (random category, 20s timer, Auto Move enabled) — filters cannot be changed while in Tournament mode.
  3. Timer: choose a timer duration or pick Timer: Off to disable timing entirely.
  4. Round flow & validation: a letter appears on the right and the category on the left. Type answers starting with that letter; live-as-you-type validation accepts correct answers instantly and shows a green checkmark for matches.
  5. Hints & reveal: press Hint to reveal progressive prefixes (live-as-you-type hints). Hints are disabled in Hard and Tournament modes — the Hint button will indicate when disabled. Press Give Up to reveal canonical and example answers.
  6. Next round & Enter: when a round ends the Hint button transforms into Next Round; you can also press Enter to advance. If Auto Move is enabled, a short countdown will advance automatically.
  7. Auto Move: enable Auto Move in Settings to automatically move to the next round after the short countdown when a round ends.
  8. Scoreboard visibility: use Show Scoreboard in Settings to hide or show the scores panel; this preference is saved to localStorage and restored on load.
  9. Scoring & readiness: streaks, best streak, correct counts and rounds appear in the score panel. Tournament Readiness (0–100) is shown in all modes and is used by competitive modes for penalties/bonuses; its impact is strongest in Hard and Tournament modes.
  10. Persistence: filters, scoreboard visibility, and scores persist to localStorage. Use Reset Scores in Settings to clear stored score data.

Tip: The gear icon (Settings) is the single place to toggle Mode, Auto Move, Show Scoreboard, Dark/Light theme, and Reset Scores.
