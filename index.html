How to use Fast Facts Simulator

1. Choose a Category, Timer, and Mode from the controls in the header.

2. A letter will appear on the right and a category will appear on the left.

3. Type answers that start with the letter into the input. Validation is live — correct answers are accepted instantly.

4. Use Hint to reveal a progressive prefix; Give Up shows the canonical answer and examples.

5. When the round ends (correct answer or time expired) the Hint button becomes Next Round.

6. Toggle Auto Move to automatically advance after a short countdown when a round finishes.
